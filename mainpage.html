justPet

JustPet.shop allows pet non-profits to connect with pet owners looking to donate their pet products. This effort aims to reduce a four-legged friend's carbon footprint as well as build a community of pet lovers.


Our Inspiration

Yoonjung, my coding organization's community director, was looking for a new home for some cat products in good hygienic condition. She gave up her products at a local Goodwill store, but I realized that there was a need for a platform that allows pet owners to connect and make a difference with green sustainable practices.

Despite the positive effects of animal companionship on physiological, psychological, and social aspects of quality of life, pet owners must acknowledge the environmental impacts and costs of owning pets. At JustPet, our mission is to build a community of pet lovers who care about the environment as well as our four-legged friends.
