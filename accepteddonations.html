Accepted Donations

JustPet is grateful for your support. Any pet items need not be in perfect condition, however they should not need any repair, have any stains, or have any infestation.

Accepted Donations

  • Rugs/carpet squares (with bound edges only, on all sides)
  • Dog treats (Pup-peroni is a shelter dog favorite) and cat treats
  • Farm Animal Feeder
  • Newspaper
  • New and gently used collars, harnesses and leashes
  • New dog and cat toys
  • Feline Milk Replacer for kittens in foster care
  • New and gently used linens (towels, blankets, bath mats, etc.)
  • Rubber cleaning gloves
  • Latex exam gloves

Restricted Donations

  • Chewed animal accessories or toys
  • Furniture with missing pieces (no feet, missing screws or bolts)
  • Stained or torn pet towels or blankets
  • Opened food or treats
